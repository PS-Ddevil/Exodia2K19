RC QUADCOPTER DRONE

Quadcopter is a fully dynamic control Vertical Takeoff and landing vehicle. This workshop introduces a host of electronic equipment which are used but focuses less on the Aeromodelling. They are known for their ability to lift heavy loads without much complex mechanics

RC FLYING WING

Flying Wing is a tail less aircraft which is the fast in speed and less in weight and drag. RC Flying Wing Workshop is perfect for those who wants to design and fly advance RC Air vehicles.

SIXTH SENSE

Six Sense is a robotics workshop which would deal with six sense technologies. It deals with controlling the robot through gestures and making different applications work without switches or buttons. Motive of the workshop is to make students familiar with sixth sense technologies and feel the augmented realism.

ANDROID APP DEVELOPMENT

Learn & Interact with renowned Industry Experts. Receive an unparalleled education on the art of computer security with personal one-on-one attention. 2 days Hand on Practical Workshop on Android Application Development. Students will get "SI-Android Tools Kit" free of cost. Android Development E-Book will also be provided to the students during the workshop. Practical Demonstration application development will be covered during the workshop. Three practical projects to be undertaken during the workshop.

VIRTUAL MOBILE

Live hands-on Sessions on “Virtual Mobile”. Topics to be covered like: Interfacing Relay with MCU, Button-less virtual wall switch, Establishing communication b/w AVR and PC, Digital image processing, Live Video Processing. Projects to be covered during the session.

ETHICAL HACKING

This workshop mainly focuses on the students eager to be a White Hat Hacker. It features a brief introduction to the world of hacking starting with the importance of ethical hackers and their need in today's world scenario, including to the basics of networking that will help you have a deep understanding of the working of internet.

ARDUINO AND SENSORS

Arduino is a tool for making computers that can sense and control more of the physical world than your desktop computer. It’s an open-source physical computing platform based on a simple microcontroller board, and a development environment for writing software for the board. It can be used to develop interactive objects, taking inputs from a variety of switches or sensors, and controlling a variety of lights, motors, and other physical outputs. Arduino projects can be stand-alone, or they can communicate with software running on your computer (e.g. Flash, Processing)

ROBO HAND

The workshop would deal with the development of Robotics Hand. Which will deal with making of structure and controlling of servo motor with laptop/desktop keyboard. This workshop is beneficial for mechanical, electronics, computer science, electrical students.

RC TRAINER AIRCRAFT

Trainer Aircraft Workshop introduces undergraduates to the science of Aeromodelling - Stability and control, build, flying etc.Trainer Workshop is perfect for those who need to explore this science/ hobby. It helps them build upon this skill and continue in the future.

RC AEROBIC AIRCRAFT

Aerobatic Plane Workshop is suggested for those who are well-versed in the basics and want a first-hand experience to application based designing of aircraft. Aerobic Aircraft is perfect for those who need to explore this science/ hobby. It helps them build upon this skill and continue in the future.

VOICE CONTROL ROBOT

This workshop is related to speech processing which will be actuated with the robot wirelessly for controlling its different actions. Usage of MATLAB for speech processing and for wireless communication usage will be with Bluetooth module. Whatever voice command you will give, it will react as same.

RC TRICOPTER

The main target of the workshop is to establish the base which will help you to independently design, build and fly a Tricopter. The course will give an introduction to basic concepts of Aerospace Engineering like control and stability of Tricopter along with the introduction to rotor dynamics.

VOICE CONTROL AUTOMATIONS

Live hands-on Sessions on Voice Control Automation using MATLAB and SIMULINK. Topics to be covered like: Automation, AVR Microcontroller, Bluetooth Communication, Signal Enhancements, Filtration, Live Speech Processing. Projects to be covered during the session. Techfleeters Infotech certification programs

ROBOT VISION

Live hands-on Sessions on “Robot Vision”. Topics to be covered like: Future of Robotics, DC Motors, Bluetooth, Digital Image Processing, Serial Communication, Machine Vision. Projects to be covered during the session

IMAGE PROCESSING AND GUI

Live hands-on Sessions on IMAGE PROCESSING & GUI Development using MATLAB and SIMULINK. It deals with sensors and their calibration to provide a sense of environment to the robot. Motive of the workshop is to make students familiar with robot automation and a mobile controlled robot too. Topics to be covered like: Image Enhancements, Morphology, Denoising, ROI, Filtration, Live Video Processing, GUI Development.
