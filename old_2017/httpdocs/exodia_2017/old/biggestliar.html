Description and Rules :

You have got yourself a chance to lie as much as you like. If you are the best, you get crowned as 'The Biggest Liar' - if not, too bad! Lie to survive. Don't forget - 'Wit beyond measure is man's greatest treasure.'

The event consists of three rounds:

1. The first round will be a surprise written elimination. 24 candidates will go forward.

2. The second round will be a rapid-fire Yes-or-No round. Top-6 from here will make it to the final round.

3. The third round will be a situation round, where a situation has to be described as per the instructions.

4. Judges can fire up questions between your narrative.
