FAQs

What is SAP ?

SAP(Student Ambassador Program) gives you a golden chance to become the face of Exodia in your college. You get an opportunity to publicise this enthralling event to ensure maximum participation and organise workshop in your college.

Who can join SAP?

Any college going student with college identity card.

How to join SAP?

Click on the register button and go ahead to join the biggest fest of Himalayas.

How many Student Ambassador can be appointed for any college?

Exodia can appoint more than one Student Ambassador from the same college on the basis of the participation of the college the the enthusiasm of the person.In case of more registration than the requirement, the selection will be done on the basis of the tasks given.

What are the requirement for earning a Certificate?

The certificate will be provided to all those who actively participated throughout the tasks and were on the same page as their mentors.

Whom should I contact for more information?

Click on the Contact Us for the info. In latter case you can contact your assigned mentor. :)
